My History.

Professionally speaking, I've dabbled in a thing or two. I have worked as an Illustrator, Graphic Designer, User Experience Designer and Front End Developer.

In mid 2014, I started Tinsy Co. my own jewelry company specializing in 3D printed miniatures of pets as charms. After about a year and a half research and experimentation with the jewelry line I felt the call of web development was too strong.

In late 2017, I dove back into web development and earned a Google Developer Scholarship. In the next 10 months I spent 100 hours of coding to catch back up to current front end technology. I was shocked to see that ES6 had been released since I was away!

Nonetheless, I perservered and secured a coveted spot in the Udacity Nanodegree program. I plan to graduate in October 2018 with a certification as a Mobile Web Developer.
