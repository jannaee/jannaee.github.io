So far to this date I have invested over 100 hours of time learning and practicing coding.

My plans for the rest of 2018 is to continue freelancing, finish the Udacity Mobile Web Specalist course and take the Google Certification so that I can become formally recognized as a Google Certified Mobile Web Specalist.
